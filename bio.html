Meet Our Team

Raghunand Dayanandan

President/Founder

As the Founder and President of Movement for Improvement, I'm passionate about creating opportunities for people, especially youth, to lead positive change through community service, physical activity, and collective action. I started this nonprofit to help others realize that even small actions, like running a mile or assisting a neighbor, can add up to something powerful when we work together.

My leadership experience began with the National Junior Honor Society, where I served as an officer. In that role, I helped organize service events, tutored fellow students, and supported initiatives encouraging academic growth and community involvement. These experiences inspired me to take my passion for service even further by launching my organization.

I'm currently a student at Jasper High School. Outside of school, I enjoy playing tennis, exploring math, and staying involved in community efforts. Whether I'm on the court or planning our next project, I'm always looking for ways to grow, give back, and bring others along with me.

Starting Movement for Improvement has been one of the most meaningful things I've done, and I'm excited to continue building a team that believes in purpose, service, and the power of movement.
